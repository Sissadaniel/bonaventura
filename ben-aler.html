Music

Ben Aler’s music hits you like that first sip of smoky mezcal. Raunchy, Led Zeppelinesque guitar tones accompanied by soulful vocals blend into a fresh, groovy, and spicy soundscapes. This heat wave of modern rock n roll is definitely a must listen.

Ben Aler hails from the creative energy of Mexico City and is currently based in Brooklyn where he recorded his upcoming EP. In the past months, Ben took over Studio G with his international band of Berklee Alumni Carlos “Cabeto” Herrand Pou, Pietro Gennenzi and Tommaso Taddonio to catch the essence of compelling live performance in 4 expressive tunes.

He just released his 3th single “Louder” which is part of his upcoming EP called “Feels Like 43”, that is expected to come out in a month.
